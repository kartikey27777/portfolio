Help & Guidelines

How does MedReturn work in real life?

Our goal is to create a safe and beneficial cycle for unused medicines. When you submit a medicine, it doesn't just disappear. Here's the real-world journey:

  • Verification: Our partner pharmacies physically inspect your returned medicine to ensure the packaging is untampered, it's not expired, and it has been stored correctly.
  • Re-dispensing: Once verified, these medicines are re-dispensed at a reduced cost to patients in need, improving healthcare accessibility. They are never resold at full price.
  • Secure Disposal: Medicines that don't pass verification are disposed of using environmentally safe methods, preventing them from contaminating our water systems.
  • Refund: Your refund is a percentage of the value recovered by re-dispensing the medicine. This is credited directly to your MedReturn Wallet.

Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

What medicines can I return?
You can return most non-prescription and prescription tablets and capsules that are in their original, sealed blister packs. We cannot accept liquids, injections, or opened medicine bottles.

Is my data secure?
Absolutely. All personal information is stored securely and is never shared with third parties.
